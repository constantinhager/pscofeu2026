# No more passwords: The future is now!

## Abstract

Credentials, Passwords, Secrets, Certificates - Imagine never needing to worry about them ever again. This is the reality that workload identity federation provides, and you can utilize it today.

In this session, we’ll take a deep dive into how workload identities work and walk through practical steps for implementing them across real-world scenarios.

By the end of this session, you’ll leave equipped to reduce risk, eliminate credential sprawl, and confidently adopt secure, passwordless authentication in your automation workflows.
