## Marc-André Moreau

<img src="https://sessionize.com/image/5685-200o200o2-QQCZWG77MV5wfBoRQAQPxR.jpg" alt="Marc-Andr&#233; Moreau" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

*CTO at Devolutions*

Marc-André Moreau is CTO at Devolutions and a Microsoft MVP in PowerShell. He is the founder of FreeRDP, the leading open-source implementation of Microsoft’s Remote Desktop Protocol, and has spent over 15 years exploring remote access technologies, Windows internals, and authentication protocols. His work focuses on building secure remote access and privileged access management solutions using Rust, C#, and PowerShell, and he regularly shares technical deep dives on remoting, protocol internals, and automation at awakecoding.com.
