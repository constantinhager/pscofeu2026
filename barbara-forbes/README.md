## Barbara Forbes

<img src="https://sessionize.com/image/6939-200o200o2-4HzpJWG1Qpx8UKbt4xJRzr.jpg" alt="Barbara Forbes" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

*Former Field CTO @ OGD | Azure MVP | GitHub Star*

Barbara Forbes is the Field CTO at OGD in the Netherlands, a Microsoft Azure MVP, Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT), and a GitHub Star. She works at the intersection of strategy, technology, education, and development. Her technical focus is on the Microsoft Cloud Adoption Framework, Generative AI, Infrastructure as Code, PowerShell, and GitHub. 
Barbara loves teaching in an accessible manner; in person, as a teacher for LinkedIn Learning and as a frequent speaker at conferences and user groups. She is actively involved in the tech community, including being part of the organization of European events
