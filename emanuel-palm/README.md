## Emanuel Palm

<img src="https://sessionize.com/image/2b97-200o200o2-fBtWJVs2wWN5V1vaYXPWgc.png" alt="Emanuel Palm" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

*Cloud Specialist, Coffee Nerd and Microsoft MVP @ Unwonted*

Emanuel is a Microsoft MVP who loves to solve problems using code. Whether it's Infrastructure-as-Code, pipeline automation or cloud native platforms, with PowerShell, Bicep and C# as his tools of choice he soars through the clouds of Azure on a daily basis.

He works at Unwonted in Sweden as a Cloud Specialist, and blogs about Azure, PowerShell, .NET and various IT adventures on https://pipe.how. He enjoys open source, roasts his own coffee, plays D&D and sometimes combines his interests into quirky apps.

Maintainer of open source tools such as the PowerShell modules PSBicep, AzAuth and AzBobbyTables.
