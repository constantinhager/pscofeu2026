# Lists, Dictionaries, or Arrays – Where the Heck Should I Store My Data?

## Abstract

You’ve got data you want to retrieve, but where should you store it? Arrays, Lists, Dictionaries… the options are many, and the decision isn’t always obvious. In the jungle of possibilities, finding the right tool can be tricky.

In this session, we’ll explore the strengths and risks of each choice, look at how performance plays a role, and discuss whether there’s a go-to approach that works in most situations. When we step further into parallelism, we’ll see why thread safety suddenly matters as well.

Even if you think you already know the answer, I’m confident we’ll uncover scenarios you may not have considered before. Join me on this adventure into the jungle of data structures and leave with a clearer map for making the right choice next time.
