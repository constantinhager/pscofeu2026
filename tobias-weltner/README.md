## Tobias Weltner

<img src="https://sessionize.com/image/66ff-200o200o2-HEQTSTNiFaWpmfcLY8iEkj.jpg" alt="Tobias Weltner" style="max-width:300px; max-height:300px; width:auto; height:auto;" />

*ISESteroids and PowerShell Expert*

Tobias started psconf.eu and ran this event from 2016-2019 in Hannover. He is in his 23rd year as a PowerShell MVP, has invented the ISESteroids extension for the ISE editor and is currently member of the Microsoft cmdlet working group. He has written more than 150 books on operating systems, security, and automation. His recent works are published by O’Reilly. As a consultant and trainer, he works for many companies and institutions. He offers public and inhouse trainings, online and offline, for beginners, experts and special interests.
