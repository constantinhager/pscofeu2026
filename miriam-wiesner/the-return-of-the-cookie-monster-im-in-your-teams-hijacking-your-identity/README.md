# The Return of the Cookie Monster: I'm in your Teams, hijacking your identity

## Abstract

Remember the Cookie Monster from last PSConf, happily munching on your Entra ID authentication tokens? This year it is back - and its appetite has grown. Now it’s after your Microsoft Teams session cookies, bypassing MFA while it impersonates users, slips into confidential chats and meetings, and pivots across the organization through trusted communication channels.

In this demo-driven session, follow the Cookie Monster as we show how a single stolen cookie can escalate into a full-blown identity takeover. See how attackers blend into collaboration workflows, abuse Teams as an internal trust engine, and silently move laterally - and learn what defenders can do to detect, disrupt, and evict cookie-powered intruders before they crumble your defenses.
