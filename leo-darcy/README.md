*Cloud Solutions Architect*

From System Center to Azure, Remote Desktop Services to Intune, Leo has built solutions for customers of every size. He has a passion for automation and has expanded into full software development where he is the creator of the Always On VPN Dynamic Profile Configurator (DPC).

In his non-working life, he is the father of an amazing daughter and enjoys setting off fireworks (with or without PowerShell)
